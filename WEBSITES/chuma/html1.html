ABOUT ME

Hey all!! This is Muthukrishnan, I'm in this field since 1994. This is my hereditary profession. I came to this profession because I always wanted to make things with my own hand, this desire made me to have a passion towards making ornaments. I always wanted to satisfy the customer needs and till now with God's grace I have achieved that. I take pride in saying that I'm specialist in making Caste stone ring . In the world of technology where people go for gunshot for piercing the ear. I do in a traditional way using needle which is much safer and less painful. Wedding chain is the true definition of marriage in Indian culture, and I make all kinds of wedding chain . Above all, it's been 25years and I'm happy that my clients are happy with my work!
